Ingredients

  • 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour (sifted)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 4 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 3/4 cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) milk (warm)
  • 1/4 cup (60g) unsalted butter (melted)
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line an 8-inch (20 cm) cake pan with parchment paper.
  2. Sift dry ingredients: In a bowl, sift the flour, baking powder, and salt together. Set aside.
  3. Whisk eggs & sugar: In a separate large bowl, beat the eggs and sugar with an electric mixer for about 5 minutes until the mixture is pale, thick, and fluffy.
  4. Add vanilla: Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Fold in dry ingredients: Gently fold in the sifted flour mixture in batches using a spatula, being careful not to deflate the batter.
  6. Add milk & butter: Mix the warm milk and melted butter together, then gently fold it into the batter.
  7. Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool & serve: Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing or frosting.
