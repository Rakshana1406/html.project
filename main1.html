Ingredients

    For the Pasta:


    250g pasta (penne, spaghetti, or fettuccine) 4 cups water 1 teaspoon salt

    For the Sauce:


    1 tablespoon butter 1 tablespoon olive oil 4 cloves garlic (minced) 1 cup heavy cream (or milk for a lighter version) ½ cup parmesan cheese (grated) ½ teaspoon black pepper ½ teaspoon salt ½ teaspoon red chili flakes (optional, for spice) 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning 2 tablespoons fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)

Instructions

    Step 1: Cook the Pasta


    Boil water in a large pot. Add salt and pasta. Cook as per package instructions (about 8-10 minutes) until al dente. Drain and set aside, reserving ½ cup of pasta water.

    Step 2: Prepare the Creamy Sauce


    Heat butter and olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant (about 30 seconds). Pour in heavy cream and stir well. Add parmesan cheese, black pepper, salt, red chili flakes, and Italian seasoning. Stir continuously until smooth.

    Step 3: Combine & Serve


    Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss well to coat. If the sauce is too thick, add some reserved pasta water to adjust consistency. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot!
