Ingredients

    For the Gravy:


  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 medium onions (chopped)
  • 2 medium tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 10-12 cashews (soaked in warm water for 10 minutes)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust consistency)

    • For the Paneer:


  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tablespoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves, crushed)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro (for garnish)

Instructions

    Step 1: Prepare the Gravy


  1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil and 1 tablespoon butter in a pan.
  2. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  5. Add tomato purée and cook for 5-7 minutes until the oil separates.
  6. Blend the soaked cashews with a little water to make a smooth paste. Add this to the pan.

    7. Step 2: Cook the Gravy


  7. Add red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, salt, and sugar. Stir well.
  8. Pour in water and simmer for 5 minutes.
  9. Blend the gravy with a hand blender (optional) for a silky texture.

    10. Step 3: Add Paneer & Finish


  10. Add paneer cubes and mix gently. Simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  11. Stir in cream, garam masala, and kasuri methi. Cook for 2 minutes.

    12. Step 4: Serve


  12. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
