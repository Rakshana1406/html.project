Ingredients

    For the Spinach Purée:


  • 250g pasta (penne, spaghetti, or fettuccine)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt

    For the Gravy:


  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream (or milk for a lighter version)
  • ½ cup parmesan cheese (grated)
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon red chili flakes (optional, for spice)
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)

Instructions

    Step 1: Blanch & Purée the Spinach


  1. Boil 2 cups of water and add spinach leaves. Blanch for 2 minutes until wilted.
  2. Transfer immediately to ice-cold water to retain the green color.
  3. Blend the spinach with 1 green chili into a smooth purée. Set aside.

    4. Step 2: Prepare the Gravy


  4. Heat oil and butter in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  5. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  6. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  7. Add tomato purée, turmeric, and coriander powder, then cook until the oil separates.

    8. Step 3: Add Spinach & Paneer


  8. Stir in the spinach purée and mix well. Add salt and garam masala.
  9. Pour in water and simmer for 3-5 minutes.
  10. Add paneer cubes and gently mix. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  11. Stir in fresh cream (optional) for a rich texture.

    12. Step 4: Serve


  12. Garnish with a drizzle of cream and serve hot with naan, roti, or jeera rice.
