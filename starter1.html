Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon whole black peppercorns (crushed slightly)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 shallot (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup beef or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup brandy or cognac (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Crush the Peppercorns – Use a mortar and pestle or the back of a spoon to slightly crush the peppercorns.
  2. Sauté the Shallots – In a pan, heat butter and olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped shallots and cook until translucent.
  3. Deglaze with Brandy (Optional) – Pour in the brandy or cognac and let it cook for about 1-2 minutes until the alcohol evaporates.
  4. Add Broth & Simmer – Stir in the broth and let it reduce slightly for about 3 minutes.
  5. Incorporate Cream & Mustard – Reduce the heat and add heavy cream and Dijon mustard. Stir well and let the sauce thicken.
  6. Season & Serve – Add salt to taste and simmer for another 2 minutes. Remove from heat and serve over steak, chicken, or roasted vegetables.
