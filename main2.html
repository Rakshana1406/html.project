Ingredients

    For the Cashew Paste:


  • 1/2 cup (75g) cashews (soaked in warm water for 15 minutes)
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) milk or water (for grinding)

    • For the Gravy:


  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 medium tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) fresh cream or milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • 1/4 cup water (adjust consistency)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro (for garnish)

Instructions

    Step 1: Prepare the Cashew Paste


  1. Blend soaked cashews with milk or water into a smooth paste. Set aside.

    2. Step 2: Cook the Gravy


  2. Heat oil and butter in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  5. Add tomato puree, turmeric, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook until the oil separates.
  6. Add cashew paste and mix well, cooking for 2-3 minutes.

    7. Step 3: Add Paneer & Finish Cooking


  7. Pour in water and salt, then add paneer cubes. Simmer for 3-5 minutes.
  8. Stir in fresh cream or milk and garam masala. Mix well and cook for another 2 minutes.
  9. Add sugar (if using) and adjust seasoning.

    10. Step 4: Serve


  10. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
