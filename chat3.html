Ingredients

    For the Puff Pastry:


  • 1 sheet store-bought puff pastry (or homemade)
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour (for dusting)
  • 2 tablespoons butter (melted, for brushing)

    • For the Paneer Filling:


  • 200g paneer (crumbled)
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 small tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Instructions

    Step 1: Prepare the Paneer Filling


  1. Boil water in a large pot. Add salt and pasta.
  2. Cook as per package instructions (about 8-10 minutes) until al dente.
  3. Drain and set aside, reserving ½ cup of pasta water.

    4. Step 2: Assemble the Puffs


  4. Heat butter and olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant (about 30 seconds).
  6. Pour in heavy cream and stir well.
  7. Add parmesan cheese, black pepper, salt, red chili flakes, and Italian seasoning. Stir continuously until smooth.

    8. Step 3: Bake the Puffs


  8. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss well to coat.
  9. If the sauce is too thick, add some reserved pasta water to adjust consistency.
  10. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot!

    11. Step 4: Serve


  11. Serve hot with green chutney or ketchup.
