Ingredients

    For Paneer Frying:


  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup water
  • Oil (for frying)


    • For the Sauce:


  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon ginger (finely chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chilli (slit)
  • 1 small onion (cubed)
  • 1 small capsicum (cubed)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon red chilli sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cornflour mixed with ¼ cup water (for thickening)
  • 1 tablespoon spring onions (chopped, for garnish)

Instructions

    Step 1: Fry the Paneer



  1. In a bowl, mix cornflour, all-purpose flour, black pepper, salt, and water to make a smooth batter.
  2. Dip the paneer cubes in the batter and coat evenly.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and deep-fry (or shallow-fry) the paneer until golden brown. Drain and set aside.

    4. Step 2: Prepare the Sauce



  4. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan. Add chopped ginger, garlic, and green chilli. Sauté for a few seconds.
  5. Add cubed onions and capsicum, stir-fry on high heat for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Pour in soy sauce, red chilli sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, black pepper, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Stir in the cornflour-water mixture and let the sauce thicken.

    8. Step 3: Assemble the Dish



  8. Add the fried paneer to the sauce and toss well to coat.
  9. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
