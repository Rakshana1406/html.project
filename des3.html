Ingredients

  • 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups (420ml) milk
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the waffle iron according to the manufacturerâ€™s instructions.
  2. Mix dry ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Prepare wet ingredients: In another bowl, beat the eggs, then add milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  4. Combine the mixtures: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir gently until just combined (do not overmix).
  5. Cook the waffles: Grease the waffle iron lightly, then pour the batter into the waffle maker and cook according to the instructions (usually 3-5 minutes) until golden brown.
  6. Serve & enjoy: Serve warm with syrup, butter, fresh fruits, or toppings of your choice.
